Lebanon Awaits Your Visit!
There are so many reasons to visit Lebanon. The country may be tiny, but it offers several attractions within its 10,452
square kilometers, making it an ideal getaway for visitors wanting to experience the best that the Middle East has to offer.

NATURAL ATTRACTIONS:
You can enjoy the beaches in Byblos, Batroun, Jiyeh and Tyre.
You can ski on the snow in Faraya, the Cedars or Laqlouq. You can also visit natural attractions
like the Jeita Grotto and the iconic Raouche rocks, off Beirut's Mediterranean coastline.

CULTURAL ATTRACTIONS:
Lebanon possesses spectatular historic, archeological and cultural sites, many of them dating back to the Roman era,
including those at Byblos, Baalbeck and Tyre. And Beirut is home to a fascinating National Museum that showcases
archeological wonders from Lebanon that are thousands of years old. Lebanon hosts international Festivals each year
in dazzling sites like Baalbeck, Beiteddine, Byblos and Tyre.

CULINARY ATTRACTIONS:
Restaurant lovers can choose from hundreds of restaurants offering, lebanese cuisine, but also international specialities.
In Beirut, as well as outside the city, whether in the mountains, in the Bekaa Valley or on the country's southern or northern
coastlines, there are French, Italien, Japanese, Mexican and various types is restaurants

NIGHTLIFE ATTRACTIONS:
Beirut nights are legendary around the world, and the city is rich with bars, pubs and nightclubs that often stay open until dawn.
Visitors seeking additional excitement can gamble the night away at the famed Casino du Liban, located on the edge of Jounieh.
